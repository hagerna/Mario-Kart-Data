The Process

In order to break up our routine and not just pick our favorites each time, I created an algorithm to randomly select the Character, Vehicle, Wheels, and Glider for each person to race with. This randomization was also done to avoid any unconscious bias in selection.

Create a loadout for yourself below!

Worth noting is that the pool for random selections has remained unchanged since its creation in order to preserve the validity of the "study". In other words, any characters, vehicles, wheels, or gliders unlocked while collecting data have not been added.
